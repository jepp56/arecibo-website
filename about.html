The Inspiration Behind Our Work

The Arecibo Message

The Arecibo Message is an interstellar radio message carrying basic information about humanity and Earth that was sent to distant space from the Arecibo Telescope in Puerto Rico in Nov 1974. It was meant as a demonstration of human technological achievement, rather than a real attempt to enter into a conversation with extraterrestrials.

We reference this impactful message to symbolize our desire to utilize the untapped potential of AI in order to remotely communicate meaningful data between the disconnected.

The Arecibo Radio

Our name and logo both are inspired by the Arecibo radio telescope, once one of the largest and most powerful radio observatories in the world. Located in Puerto Rico, it was built to listen for faint signals from deep space, unlocking discoveries about the unknown.

In the same spirit, Arecibo AI is built to listen deeply to the often-overlooked signals in care-giving and cognitive health. We believe that meaningful care starts with paying attention—quietly, intelligently, and with empathy.
