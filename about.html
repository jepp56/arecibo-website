Our Story

Our story began in 2024. Motivated by his own personal care-giving journey, Benny Esparra partnered with Dr. Yanshan and a team of interns to build Arecibo AI, laying the roots for smarter elder care

The Inspiration Behind Our Work

Before his grandparents recently passed away from dementia, Benny and his family members had the privilege and burden of caring for them while they shared stories of their 72-year marriage.

Their storytelling connected the family, including the 75 grandchildren, to the aging patriarch and matriarch supporting their quality of life and care. It inspired Benny and Yanshan to create a conversational AI companion which engages with elders and transforms those life-stories into meaningful health insights for caregivers.

The Arecibo Inspiration

Our name and logo both are inspired by the Arecibo radio telescope, once one of the largest and most powerful radio observatories in the world. Located in Puerto Rico, it was built to listen for faint signals from deep space, unlocking discoveries about the unknown.

Just as NASA’s Arecibo Telescope transmitted a story of humanity into deep space in the 1970’s, Arecibo AI connects humans to our technological story across the widening gap of the AI space.
